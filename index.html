Xiaoxue Wu is a lecturer in School of Information Engineering at Yangzhou University. She received her Ph.D. degree at School of Cyberspace Security, University of Northwestern Polytechnical, China. She received the M.Sc. degree in Software Engineering from University of Northwestern Polytechnical, China. B.E. degree in Information Management from University of Xi Dian, China. Her main research interest includes intelligent software testing, mining software repository and software vulnerability analysis. She has pubished over 20 articles in journals, conferences, and book chapters.

吴潇雪，博士，讲师，中国计算机学会(CCF)会员。2021年6月毕业于西北工业大学网络空间安全学院，获工学博士学位。2021年7月起在扬州大学信息工程学院任教。 研究兴趣包括智能化软件测试、软件仓库挖掘、软件安全漏洞演化分析等。以第一作者/通讯作者发表SCI期刊论文10多篇，CCF-A/CCF-B类期刊论文8篇。
